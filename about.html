I have written most of these poems on my phone. I tend to make a few edits after the initial "outburst", usually over the course of a few days. If I find myself making too many edits or returning to a poem for a prolonged period, I get disgruntled.

The idea to create this kind of website -- with a randomly selected poem appearing each time the page is refreshed -- came to me in early 2020. I think I was in the kitchen. I soon found some JavaScript that would work for the purpose. Sometimes you may see the same poem appearing after a page refresh. Hopefully the incidence of this happening will reduce as the number of poems increases.

The numbering of the poems is random, as the poems themselves are, and as the triggers or itches that caused them.

This website has a total of 22 poems as of 18 October 2022.
